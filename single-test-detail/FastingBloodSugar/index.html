Accelerating Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS) Analysis with Infiplus Pathology Lab Software

At Infiplus, we create the best custom software for Pathology Labs, ensuring seamless operations and enhanced diagnostic accuracy. Our solution transforms the handling of fundamental tests like the Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS), providing pathologists and clinicians with powerful tools for immediate assessment and diabetic risk reporting.

The Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS) test is the primary tool for screening for prediabetes and diabetes by measuring glucose levels after a patient has fasted overnight. It's essential for rapid and accurate diagnosis of metabolic disorders. Our software is specifically designed to manage FBS results with precision, utilizing Auto Calculations for related indices (like estimated average glucose) and featuring AI detection to flag results requiring immediate attention, guaranteeing fast and reliable diagnostic support.

Infiplus Software: Reliability for Metabolic Diagnostics

Infiplus provides the best custom software for Pathology Labs, meticulously designed to handle critical tests like the FBS with ease and accuracy. Our solution integrates every essential feature your lab needs for seamless operation and superior diagnostic support.

Core Features for Enhanced Lab Efficiency:

  • Reduce Error with Auto Calculations: Automatically calculate related glucose indices and conversions, guaranteeing precise, error-free results for metabolic profiles.
  • Inbuilt AI & Recommendation Engine: Our embedded AI analyzes FBS results against reference ranges, predicts diabetic risk, and suggests relevant follow-up tests or lifestyle recommendations for the patient.
  • Powerful Report Customizations: Design the FBS report format to clearly highlight critical glucose levels, complete with authorized Signature at the end of reports for professional validation.
  • Comparison and Combine Reporting: Generate Comparison reports to track glucose trends over time, and use Combine reports to integrate FBS results with Lipid Profile results for a comprehensive metabolic overview.
  • Prefilled Interpretations: Accelerate reporting with pre-validated, editable interpretations for normal, prediabetic, and diabetic FBS ranges, ensuring consistency.
  • Send Reports Online: Securely and instantly deliver final FBS reports to patients and doctors via your dedicated online platform, email, or SMS.

Infiplus is committed to providing pathology labs with the best custom software solution to manage the high volume and complexity of tests like the Fasting Blood Sugar test.

Accelerating Arterial Blood Gas (ABG) Analysis with Infiplus Pathology Lab Software

At Infiplus, we create the best custom software for Pathology Labs, ensuring seamless operations and enhanced diagnostic accuracy. Our solution transforms the handling of urgent and complex tests like the Arterial Blood Gas (ABG), providing pathologists and critical care clinicians with powerful tools for immediate patient status assessment.

The ABG test is a critical measure of blood pH, oxygen $\text{pO}_2$, and carbon dioxide $\text{pCO}_2$ levels, essential for evaluating pulmonary function and the body's acid-base balance. Given the urgency of ABG results, our software is specifically designed for speed and precision. It utilizes Auto Calculations for derived values like Bicarbonate ($\text{HCO}_3^-$) and Base Excess, and features AI detection to instantly flag life-threatening acid-base disturbances, guaranteeing rapid and reliable diagnostic support.

Infiplus Software: Precision for Critical Care Diagnostics

Infiplus provides the best custom software for Pathology Labs, meticulously designed to handle critical tests like ABG with ease and accuracy. Our solution integrates every essential feature your lab needs for seamless operation and superior diagnostic support.

Core Features for Enhanced Lab Efficiency:

  • Reduce Error with Auto Calculations: Instantly and accurately calculate derived ABG parameters like $\text{HCO}_3^-$ and Anion Gap, eliminating complex manual calculations.
  • Inbuilt AI & Recommendation Engine: Our embedded AI analyzes the full ABG panel, immediately predicts the type of acid-base disorder (e.g., respiratory acidosis), and suggests relevant next steps for treatment or goods.
  • Powerful Report Customizations: Customize the ABG report layout to clearly display critical alert ranges, ensuring the authorized Signature at the end of reports is included for compliance.
  • Comparison and Combine Reporting: Generate Comparison reports to track rapid changes in a patient's $\text{pO}_2$ and $\text{pCO}_2$ levels, vital for monitoring acute respiratory status.
  • Prefilled Interpretations: Accelerate urgent reporting with pre-validated, editable interpretations for common respiratory and metabolic states, ensuring consistent quality.
  • Send Reports Online: Securely and instantly deliver crucial ABG results to ICU/ER teams, enabling immediate clinical action.

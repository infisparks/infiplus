Hospital Software

InfiPlus Services Hospital Software

The Digital Foundation for Comprehensive Hospital Management.

Infiplus’s Complete Hospital Software is an integrated Hospital Management System (HMS) designed to manage every facet of your facility. It unifies operations from patient entry to final billing, ensuring a seamless flow across all departments: OPD, IPD, Operation Theater (OT), Inventory, and Staff Management. This comprehensive platform eliminates data silos and provides real-time control.

Our commitment is to enhance operational efficiency and patient experience. The system is built with future-ready technology, featuring Inbuilt AI for administrative optimization and WhatsApp API integration for instant communication. Our marketing professionals are trained to provide the best customer satisfaction and continuous support to ensure successful implementation and adoption across your organization.

Patient Lifecycle Management

Centralized data for accurate admissions, OPD/IPD tracking, history management, and streamlined discharge processes.

Integrated Billing & Accounts

Automated bill generation, claims processing, and transparent revenue cycle management integrated with OPD/IPD modules.

Staff & Resource Allocation

Dynamic staff scheduling, accurate leave tracking, and efficient resource allocation, all managed via a central HR module.

OPD/IPD and Bed Management

Efficiently manage outpatient and inpatient flows, including automated bed allocation, room transfers, and real-time occupancy status updates.

Core Operational Benefits

Our HMS ensures a flawless operating environment by streamlining complex administrative tasks, allowing healthcare professionals to dedicate more time to patient care.

  • Unified digital records for faster diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Automated billing and insurance claim processing to reduce financial errors.
  • AI-driven insights for optimizing resource use and reducing operational costs.
  • Secure, role-based access to patient data ensuring compliance and confidentiality.
